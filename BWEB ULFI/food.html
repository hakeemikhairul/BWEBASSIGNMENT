Ambuyat

Ambuyat is one of Brunei's national dishes.

It is a food that is created from sago

It has a sticky texture but tasted very good when you dipped it in cacah sauce. Ambuyat can be eaten withou chewing and even some people just swallows it.

This dish gets a must try medal because it is very good.


Roti Kacang Kahwin

This Roti Kacang Kahwin can be found in a well known local breakfast restaurant that is called Chop Jin Chew.

Their roti kuning is also very good and gets a must try medal as it is very delicious

Overall, this restaurant gets a five star rating because of the roti and foods that they serves.


Ayam Tongkeng

It is a chicken body part that's grilled.

This food can be found in Gadong Night Market.

Gadong Night Market is a must visit place in Brunei. If you miss this place then its not a complete trip. In this night market you can get almost anything.

The Gadong Night Market is heaven for the food lovers and also the price is very cheap.


Nasi Katok

It is basically rice with fried chicken and also sambal

But, for this $1 meal you are getting a treat.

This 'Nasi Katok' has evolved over they years into many different version.

This is a must try food also.


Pulut Panggang

It is one of the most famous Brunei snack thats grilled.

The insides usually consist of chicken or beef wrapped with rice just like kelupis.


Satay

Satay is like chicken skewer.

It is grilled with a special sauce and also served with its specific sauce.


Currypuff Factory

This is one of the best resturant that is local.

They serve currypuffs and from our opinion, it is better than Old Klang's.

They also serve other other foods eg.Nasi Katok and it is also very good.

The restaurant branches are located in Gadong and also in Kiulap.


Honey Garlic Chicken Pizza

This pizza is actually from Pizza Hut.

Brunei is actually the only place in the world that serves Honey Garlic Chicken Pizza from Pizza Hut. Hence, it is a must try pizza.
